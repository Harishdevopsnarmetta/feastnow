💡 Money-Maximizing Strategies

⚡

Chase Surge Pricing

Open the driver app and watch the heat map. Position yourself at the EDGE of a surge zone — you get the surge rate when the trip starts, even if you drive out of the zone.

+30–200% per trip
✈️

Airport Strategy

Airport pickups pay ₹2,000–4,500+. Check flight arrivals and time your queue. Use the airport staging lot and wait for arrival waves. 2–3 airport trips = your whole daily goal.

$25–$50 per trip
🎯

Accept Rate Strategy

Keep acceptance rate above 85% to maintain Pro status. But DECLINE short trips under ₹150 during surge — low earnings drag down your hourly rate dramatically.

Protects hourly rate
🏟️

Events = Big Money

Check local event calendars: concerts, sports games, festivals. Be there 30 min before it ends. Post-event surge can hit 3–5x. One concert night can equal 2 regular days.

2–5x surge possible
⭐

5-Star = More Requests

Higher ratings = priority dispatch. Keep car clean, offer phone charger, use AC/heat. 4.9+ rating unlocks Uber Pro perks including gas discounts and priority support.

More trips per hour
⛽

Cut Fuel Costs

Use GasBuddy app to find cheapest gas near you. Fill up on Mondays or Tuesdays — cheapest days. Every ₹500 saved in fuel = ₹500 straight profit. Big deal over a month.

Save $100+/month
